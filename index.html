DIGICOM

Digicom is a multidisciplinary and associative event held for the first time by cse as a response to the tendancy performed by businesses, start-ups therefore companies for innovative solutions and valid decisions. It covers many aspects of computer science applied to globulize the digitalisation, including preparing and designing platforms and user experience to managing the datas. It will be held in 3 days. The program will include 2 conferences about marketing and idealisation, workshops, and a competition at the end to put on mouvement the informations learnt by participants.

About the event

The "DigiComm" is a new event that is directly related to e-commerce between companies, their customers and the sponsors of the products marketed with the aim of creating new technological solutions in order to facilitate exchanges and ensure good communication between them to provide them with an excellent buying and selling process that can satisfy the needs of each of them without wasting time, preserving our health through work on the internet, to achieve these objectives our event includes sinteresting conferences, workshops, and a competition.

About Us

20 h 24 CSE -Club Scientifique de l'ESI- is a student club at the Higher National School of Computer Science, Algiers. It is created for computer science, high technology, robotics, design and anything else technology-related enthusiasts. CSE's activities made it establish itself on a national & international scale and became one of the country's largest & most active clubs.
